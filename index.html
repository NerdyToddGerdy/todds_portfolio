My Bio

Todd Gerdy is a full stack web developer, completing the General Assembly Web Development Immersive Program in June, 2017. He studied technical theatre at Longwood University and worked on set construction in summer stock theater and on the HBO series, John Adams. For the past decade, he has worked as Production Supervisor and Director of Shop Operations at Blue Ridge A/V and Lighting in Charlottesville, Virginia, while taking courses in Electronics and Computer Technology at Piedmont Virginia Community College. In his educational, work and programming experiences, he has been successful at collaboratively and creatively planning and problem solving, as part of a team, in order to provide for and satisfy the specific and diverse needs of clients.
